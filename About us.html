Introduction to the business

The name of the business is “Learning with Love” which is a Tutoring business that offers educational classes through Out-school, this business aims to offer affordable group classes for children from 5-11.

This business offers lessons for Maths, English, Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning.

Teacher's Skills

The Lecturer is going to be in the UK (United Kingdom) but there will be classes suitable for all time zones. The lecturer has a BA and a MA degree in Primary Education and has a particular interest in Early Years, Maths, English, History, Geography and the 11 Plus Exam.

The lecturer is particularly passionate about good phonics teachings for young children and creating a love of learning that will carry them through life.
